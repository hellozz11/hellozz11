Selling houses, layoffs, pay cuts, blockchain entrepreneurs laughed and survived

News Blockchain 2018/10/10

The shadow of defeat is shrouded over the blockchain. People are confused, lamented, and even fleeing, but no one records the entrepreneurs who are still sticking to the blockchain industry.

The past has passed, and the future is still far away. The only thing that can be grasped is the present.

The blockchain entrepreneurs still stationed in the bear market, they laughed and survived.

In 2018, the global blockchain industry increased by more than 50%, and the industry’s compound annual growth rate reached 42.8%. The cryptocurrency based on smart contracts flooded into the market, and the market circulated nearly 1,700 cryptocurrencies.

In the nine years since its inception, the blockchain has burst out in this year and has fallen like a meteor.

Bitcoin plunged from a high of $18,000 to $6,400. The average break rate of the three exchanges was over 90%, and the high-rise buildings built by the bubble began to collapse.

The bear market is unexpected.

But we only saw the beauty of autumn leaves, but we could not see the gorgeousness of summer flowers.

The giants accompanying the cold winter, the half-year net profit of more than 700 million US dollars, the mainland submitted a prospectus on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange, the encryption mining industry has maintained a thousand-fold growth rate for four years, the global trading center of the fire currency landed in Hainan, the currency Ann started the blockchain charity foundation…

But more ordinary blockchain entrepreneurs, selling houses to pay their own employees, more ordinary blockchain practitioners, paying a lower salary to hold on to their own technology beliefs.

There is a lot of consensus in the blockchain world, and this consensus is to live.

Selling a house to pay

“The bear market is a good time to get on the bus. Blockchain is not a cusp, but a big trend that can be compared to the Internet.”

From 6 am to 12 midnight, Zhou Kai has been working for more than half a year. This high-intensity work rhythm seems to be somewhat out of tune compared to the cold currency of the currency.

The market circulated less than half of the funds. The company was on the verge of bankruptcy liquidation on several occasions. In order to maintain sufficient cash flow to maintain the company’s operation, Zhou Kai mortgaged the property and used the accumulated money for many years. The mount also cost one million. Tesla replaced the Audi with 150,000.

The block turnover rate in the blockchain industry is as high as 80%, but Zhou Kai has chosen to go against the trend.

“The bull market makes money, the bear market earns experience. The bear market is an opportunity for you to try your mistakes. The turnover rate is high. The company has more choices and recruits suitable candidates at a more reasonable price.”

In July and August, Bitcoin fell nearly two-thirds from the high point, and Ethereum fell below $200 in an unprecedented way. The whole industry was filled with panic. At this time, Zhou Kai entered the blockchain and started a wallet project.

In the early years, relying on being a mobile Internet software service provider, Zhou Kai earned the first pot of gold. In another friend who also works as a B-end service, he has to cut down all the business related to the blockchain. Zhou Kai has chosen to stick to it.

Every industry will experience a process from appearance to touting, from the bursting of the bubble to the reorganization of the peak. Bear markets or early markets need to be more trial-and-error, and adjust their rhythm at any time based on market reactions. If you make a mistake in the bull market, it will be difficult to turn over.

The most important thing is the initial heart. “If you only want to get a wave of money, love Western Europe is not faster, why bother to spend a few months to bite the product.”

Did not eat half of the meal, Zhou Kai received a call from the product manager, did not hesitate, he put down the chopsticks in his hands and rushed to the R & D department, as usual.
