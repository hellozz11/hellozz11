What kind of experience is toothache?

knowledge Medical 2018/10/11

In the dentistry, there are many sources of pain in the teeth. In which cases there will be toothache, we pick some common ones to see ~

Sensitive teeth

Sensitivity to teeth is also a form of generalized “pain.” Sensitive teeth, in fact, many people have felt: the lightest, contact with cold food, biting teeth will appear; slightly heavier, cold wind will have similar sensitive symptoms.

How does tooth sensitivity occur? Because periodontitis causes tooth decay, tooth sensitivity may occur, because brushing too much force may cause tooth sensitivity in the neck of the tooth, or because the tooth may be excessively worn or even broken, the sensitivity of the tooth may be caused.

If the tooth is sensitive, if you are light, you can buy a desensitizing toothpaste and apply the desensitized toothpaste on sensitive teeth. If you have serious symptoms, you need to find a doctor. You can use desensitizing agent for desensitization treatment, even if there is a tooth. The defect can also be prevented by filling the teeth and making the crown.

Tooth decay

Because the teeth are not cleaned and eaten too much sugar…there is a tooth decay that begins to come out. Tooth decay caused by tooth decay is very common, often after eating sweets and hot and cold stimuli. However, a simple tooth decay has a characteristic that when the stimulus disappears, the pain symptoms disappear.

If it is a toothache caused by tooth decay, the dentist will remove the tooth decay and fill the missing tooth with the filling material. This will isolate the external stimulus and the tooth will not hurt.

Pulpitis

Pulpitis, often referred to as “inflammation of the dentate nerves”, is the infection of the pulp in the center of the tooth, which contains the nerves that transmit the sensation. As for how the pulp is infected, it is often caused by excessive tooth decay or severe periodontitis and tooth trauma.

The pain caused by pulpitis may be the most severe of many toothaches, and the pain caused by pulpitis is also very typical: pain often occurs at night or worse at night; and is very sensitive to hot and cold stimuli, The difference between tooth decay and tooth sensitivity is that when the stimulus is removed, the toothache still persists. What is even more troublesome is that the pain can’t be positioned sometimes, that is, we can’t feel which tooth is hurting ourselves – even up and down It’s unclear; when the pulpitis progresses to its worst, there will be divergence pain - not just teeth, gums, but even ears, eyes, chin, and neck.

When it comes to the treatment of pulpitis, root canal treatment is already a very mature treatment for pulpitis. If severe tooth pain occurs, the first choice is to find a dentist for root canal treatment. The use of any medicine can not cure the inflammation of the teeth. However, because tooth neuralgia often occurs in the evening, there is no doctor at night - so painkillers may be effective (although the effect is average), after this night, hurry to the dentist the next day!

Wisdom tooth

The pain caused by wisdom teeth is also very common. The wisdom tooth is the third molar of human being, which is located in the deepest part of the mouth. Because it often can’t grow smoothly, Changchun is a commonplace in modern people - so the local hygiene is very poor, the gums will be red and swollen, and even the formation of abscesses, after a long time, it is easy to cause tooth decay, pulpitis, etc. Happening.

After discovering that the wisdom tooth is long, it is almost necessary to remove this wisdom tooth. But before the pain has been removed, we still have measures to relieve first - the first is antibacterial drugs, the rational use of antibacterial drugs can make the inflamed gums return to normal in a short period of time; secondly, more mouthwash, brushing, only local Hygiene is done, and swelling of the gums will be eliminated.

Of course, no matter how you take medicine or brush your teeth, it is not a cure. For the wisdom tooth of the long scorpion, that is, the impacted tooth, it can only be removed by surgical methods, and it can be done once and for all.

There are many sources of toothache, but the above are the most common. Toothache is very uncomfortable, but only when you find the right way can you eliminate the pain. Many people have all sorts of strange pain-relieving methods. What kind of pepper, rice vinegar, are actually some remedies that are useless, can really relieve pain, and basically take dental treatment to remove the cause, or emergency use of painkillers.
