Rapid rise in interest rates brings harm

In fact, the general rise in US interest rates is causing harm. As the price of public debt fell, the US Treasury yield rose to 3.2594% on the 9th, hitting a seven-year high, causing market anxiety that US corporate profit margins may be subject to higher labor costs and Compression of higher borrowing costs. Driven by the rise in public debt yields, the 30-year mortgage interest rate has risen above 5%, affecting the willingness of Americans to buy homes, and auto loans and other debt interest rates have also risen.

Charlie Ripley, strategist at Allianz Investment Management, said, “The US stock market sell-off on Wednesday was a panic reaction for investors, and finally realized that we were in a higher interest rate environment, and considering that stocks came to high levels, market investors are finding out the goods. Reasons. Higher interest rates usually lead to more severe financial conditions, which may inhibit future growth, and the stock market reacts.

Funds retreat from rising technology stocks to value stocks

Observers said that the killing of large technology stocks indicates that investors have begun to sell this year’s winners and transfer funds to other stocks.
