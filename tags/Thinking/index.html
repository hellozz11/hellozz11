The Formation and Establishment of Marx and Engels' Socialist Democratic Thought

• 2018年10月11日

In the past, the academic transformation of Marx and Engels’s thoughts was generally expressed as “transition from idealism to materialism and from revolutionary democracy to communism.” When discussing such “two transformations”, I neglected that Marx and Engels had a clear and clear socialist democratic thought when they became communists. In fact, Marx and Engels inherited the outstanding achievements of ancient Greek and modern democratic thoughts and doctrines, formed socialist democratic thoughts in the process of transitioning from revolutionary democracy to communism, and created in the process of creating scientific socialism.
